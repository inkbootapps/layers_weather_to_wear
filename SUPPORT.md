## What is Layers?

## How recommendations work

Layers looks at:
- temperature (current, high, low)
- wind
- precipitation chance
- sun / UV
- time of day

From this, it suggests clothing **layers** (e.g. base, mid, outer, accessories) rather than specific brands or outfits.

Recommendations are meant to be:
- **practical**, not fashionable
- **conservative**, not risky
- easy to adapt to personal preference

You can adjust how warm or cool you run in **Settings**.
