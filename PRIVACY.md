# Privacy Policy — Layers: Weather to Wear

**Last updated:** February 2026

Layers: Weather to Wear (“Layers”) is designed to be useful without collecting unnecessary personal data. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

---

## Information we collect

Layers does **not** require you to create an account and does **not** collect personally identifiable information.

Depending on how you use the app, the following information may be used:

### Location (optional)
- If you enable **device location**, Layers uses your current location to provide local weather and clothing recommendations.
- Location data is used **only on your device** to fetch weather information.
- Location history is **not stored**, tracked, or shared.

You can disable device location at any time and use saved or manually searched locations instead.

---

## Information we do **not** collect

Layers does **not** collect:
- names, email addresses, or contact details
- account or login information
- advertising identifiers
- browsing behavior or analytics for advertising
- location history or movement tracking

---

## Weather data

Weather information is retrieved from third-party weather data providers.

- Requests may include approximate location coordinates or city names.
- These requests are used solely to retrieve weather data.
- No personal identifiers are attached to these requests.

---

## Data storage

- App preferences (such as temperature units or comfort tuning) are stored **locally on your device**.
- No personal data is stored on Layers servers.
- No data is sold or shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

---

## Widgets

If you use home screen widgets:
- Widgets display weather and clothing recommendations based on your selected or device location.
- Widgets may update less frequently due to system limitations.
- Widgets do not collect additional data beyond what the main app uses.

---

## Children’s privacy

Layers does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.  
The app contains no targeted content or advertising.

---

## Security

Layers follows platform best practices to ensure data is handled securely.  
Because minimal data is collected, risk exposure is intentionally low.

---

## Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated as the app evolves.  
Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date.

## Summary

- No account required
